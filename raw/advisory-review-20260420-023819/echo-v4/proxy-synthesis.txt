### Notes on Other Security-Relevant Changes

The following items appear in the upstream CHANGELOG but do **not** have publicly assigned CVEs or GHSAs scoped to this package. They are noted here for awareness only and are **not** promoted to the Known Vulnerabilities table:

- **v4.11.3**: Header value escaping improvements (potential response-splitting hardening).
- **v4.13.0**: Removal of built-in JWT middleware (users directed to `github.com/labstack/echo-jwt`); reduces attack surface of the core module.
- Various releases include dependency security bumps (transitive).
