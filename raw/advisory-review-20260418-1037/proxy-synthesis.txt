### Security Posture Summary

multer has accumulated **7 publicly disclosed high-severity vulnerabilities**, all in the denial-of-service category. Every vulnerability has been fixed in a released version. The current latest (`2.1.1`) resolves all known advisories. The fix history spans five releases over two major-version generations (2.0.0 → 2.1.1), suggesting active but reactive security maintenance.

**All 7 vulnerabilities are rated High severity. All are DoS vectors.** No remote-code-execution, data-exfiltration, or authentication-bypass issues appear in the public advisory corpus.
